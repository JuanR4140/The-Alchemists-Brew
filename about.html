Meet The
Founder

The founder of this company, Corinne Martinez, dreamed
of a place where she could let her thoughts run wild.
A sanctuary of dreams and wishes, somewhere she could
express her creativity with others who had she same dreams
as she did. Which is what led her to create The Alchemist’s
Brew! Here, our mission is to keep the magic of fiction alive
for all to enjoy. Through fantasy-realm inspired treats, fun
community events, board games and Dungeons & Dragons,
this establishment has only one goal in mind: To share the
warmth and joy that fiction has brought to it’s owner and
employees for all those who seek it.

 “Even the smallest people can change the
course of the future.” - Galadriel, The Fellowship Of The Ring
